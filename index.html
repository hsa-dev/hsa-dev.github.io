who we are

Our management team is comprised of Harvard's leading engineers, designers, and technologists. Find out more about the team behind HSA Dev below.

Joey Caffrey '18, Managing Director
Joey came to Harvard because he was very interested in entrepreneurship and all the amazing things happening at the i-Lab. Before long though, he realized the technological roadblock that exist for the many people who, like himself, wanted to start companies but did not have coding experience. This drove him to become a computer science major and the hope to lower the technological barrier of entry into the startup world at Harvard is what makes him so excited about HSA Dev. When not working at Dev or avoiding essays, you can find him playing ping pong, cycling, or swimming.
